Introduction

Gang Wars is a text-based game where 2 gangs from the opposite side of the city (East side and West side) are fighting each other to rule the city. At the beginning of the game, you will have to choose which gang you will join. After that, you will start at your gang side territory and you will have to move through the maps where you can find useful objects (weapons, medical objectsâ€¦) and friendly gang members that can help you or enemies that will try to kill you.
